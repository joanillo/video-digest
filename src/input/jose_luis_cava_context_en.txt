José Luis Cava is a financial analyst and investment educator, widely known on YouTube and in financial media for his opinions on the stock market, macroeconomics, and financial markets.

He provides investment strategies and possible market scenarios, focusing mainly on technical analysis and macroeconomics. He promotes financial education aimed at traders and retail investors.

In his videos, several recurring ideas appear:

-Markets are emotional: he believes the stock market is driven mainly by greed and fear, not only by economic data.
-Importance of liquidity: for him, the key factor in markets is liquidity (money flowing in and out from central banks).
-Criticism of the monetary system: he is critical of the current fiat money system (euro, dollar) and argues that there is excessive debt and monetary expansion.
-Safe-haven assets: he often recommends gold, Bitcoin, and high-quality stocks as protection.
-Trading and technical analysis: he supports the use of trading strategies. He does not believe in perfectly predicting the market, but rather in working with probabilities.
